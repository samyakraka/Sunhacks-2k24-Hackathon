PRAMAN PORTAL

Empower your hiring process with our AI-powered platform. Streamline resume collection, effortlessly match candidates to job descriptions, and find the perfect fit for your team with ease.

FEATURES

Resume Collection

Allow job applicants to submit their resumes through the platform, either by uploading a file or filling out a form. Collect relevant information such as work experience, education, skills, and certifications.

Job Description Analysis

Utilize Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze job descriptions and extract key requirements, qualifications, and skills needed for each job opening.

Accurate Job Matching

Experience a tailored job search with our machine learning-based matching algorithm, which compares your resume against job descriptions and provides you with relevant opportunities based on your skills and qualifications.

Filtering and Ranking

Automatically filter resumes based on their relevance scores and rank them in descending order of suitability for the job. Present the top-ranked resumes to recruiters or hiring managers for further review.
